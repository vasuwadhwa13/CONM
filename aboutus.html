Why Study Computer-Oriented Numerical Methods?

In today's digital world, computers handle many types of math problems (or mathematical operations) to solve real-life challenges. From scientific research and engineering design to business analytics and everyday calculations, computers help process large amounts of numerical data. Understanding how computers represent and manipulate numbers is essential for ensuring accuracy in calculations.
